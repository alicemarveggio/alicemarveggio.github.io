Alice Marveggio

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at the Institute of Science and Technology Austria (ISTA) under the supervision of Prof. Julian Fischer. From October 2023 I will be a Hausdorff postdoc at the Hausdorff Center for Mathematics (HCM) under the mentorship of Prof. Tim Laux.
My research interests lie at the intersection of partial differential equations and calculus of variations, with a particular focus on interface evolution problems arising in continuum mechanics.
